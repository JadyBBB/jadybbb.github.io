My Violin Experience



In this section I want to share with you some songs I have played on the violin. I started to play the violin when I was 6 years old because my friend started playing. I played for a while and then I stopped. Then, when I was 14, I started playing the violin again! I have been playing since then.

I am currently on level 5 of the Royal Conservatory of Music (RCM) for violin. There are 8 levels in total for the violin.

I have LOTS of room for improvement on the violin (as you will likely hear from my music below) but I love learning new patterns and techniques. And, although I am technically doing RCM, my favourite things to play are fun pieces that have nothing to do with RCM. I like finding Taylor Swift violin sheet music online and trying to play those songs. I also have a fiddle book filled with jigs, reels, and hornpipes. I love playing these songs especially because I find them really happy and fun.

So I have included a couple of songs I have played on the violin below. I hope you enjoy!
